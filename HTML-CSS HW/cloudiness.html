Latitude vs. Cloudiness

This plot graphically displays the relationship between latitude and average cloudiness. It is difficult to determine a relation between the two variables. The climate at certain latitudes does not show influence on cloudiness, as most of the data is randomly distributed with clusters of points forming at 0 and 80 percent cloudiness.
